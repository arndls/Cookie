Types of Cookies!

Chocolate Chip Cookies!

It's a classic! Nothing's better than a good old-fashioned chocolate chip cookie! Made with chocolate chips, brown sugar, and lots of butter, the chocolate chip cookie is the perfect little bundle of happiness ready to be consumed! Heartwarming with every bite, it never fails to pleases!

Oatmeal Raisin Cookies!

These are the evilest cookies! Be careful when encountering oatmeal raisin cookies. They contain raisins in them, which tastes yucky and makes the tummy feel weird. What makes the oatmeal raisin cookie so tricky, is its sneakiness. They are commonly mistaken for the much yummier chocolate chip cookies, causing many dissapointments around the world.

Sugar Cookies!

Sugar cookies are simple, sugary, sweet delights. Sugar cookies are made with white sugar, and commonly have stuff on top of them. For instance, different variations of frosting, candy, more sugar, etc. Whenever you need a cookie for yum times, a sugar cookie is a reliable source for sweets and feel goods.

Snickerdoodle Cookies!

Snickerdoodle cookies are funny! Their names are really funky and fun to say. Snickerdoodles are similar to sugar cookies, but the white sugar is used with cinnamon. Cinammon is a big part of the snickerdoodle, defining it and making it unique and special from other cookies.

Peanut Butter Cookies!

Peanut butter cookies are really nice, because they got peanut butter! Peanut butter makes a lot of things very nice. Like the name suggests, peanut butter cookies have peanut butter as a main ingredient. Often times, peanut butter cookies would also have little patterns etched into them. Peanut butter cookies are great for a mouthful of peanutty buttery feels goods!

French Macarons!

French macarons are very fancy cookies. Macarons are made out of egg whites, almond, etc. The french macaron look, taste, and feel very nice, and it brings upon a new level of elegance and fanciness upon anyone who eats them. Macarons also come in many extravagant colors, which make them really cool to look at.
